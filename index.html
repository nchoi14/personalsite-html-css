A Bit about Me

Hi everyone, and welcome to my website! I'm Nadia Choi, a junior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I’m majoring in Psychology and Information Science with a focus on UX Design. I'm also completing certificates in Digital Studies and Art Studio. My passions include Digital Marketing, Media Production, User Experience Design, Research, and Human-Computer Interaction. I’m dedicated to creating intuitive, user-friendly digital experiences that make technology accessible to all. Feel free to explore my About Me and Portfolio pages to learn more about me and my work!
